Match
Rules: You have 10 seconds to memorize as many cards as possible. The objective is to match all the cards! Once all the cards on the table have been matched the game is over. *note: There is more than one way to achieve score multipliers!

Rules: Simple once the timer expires the computer will flip cards in a random sequence. Once the computer is done, it's your job to copy the computer's moves.
